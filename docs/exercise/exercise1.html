Question 1

In the lectures, we learn from slide 40 of lecture 1 that the thickness of a gas or oil column can be retained by \[ \begin{equation} z_g = \frac{2 \gamma \cos\theta (1/r_{cap}-1/r_{res})}{g (\rho_w-\rho_g)} \label{thick} \end{equation} \] Assume:

  1. caprock is in micropores, reservoir rock is in macropores (follow table 2 of https://link.springer.com/content/pdf/10.1007/s13202-021-01298-9.pdf)
  2. the contact angle \(\theta\) is \(60\) degress
  3. the interfacial tension is \(30\) mN/m
  4. gravity acceleration constant is \(9.8\) m/s\(^2\)
  5. water density is \(1000\) kg/m\(^3\), super critical CO\(_2\) density is \(776\) kg/m\(^3\)

What is the estimated range of \(z_g\)?
