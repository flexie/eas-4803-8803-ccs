Additional material¶

In addition to the general text 1, the course will review journal papers/articles including

  • Papers on Sleipner 3 4 5
  • Coupling seismic to fluid flow 6
  • Overview article 7 and this excellent overview paper from the UK Royal Society
  • Paper on (perception of) risk 8 of CCS
  • Reservoir Model Design 9
  • Irregular Is What’s Regular for CO2 Storage Plumes
  • The carbon capture crux: Lessons learned and Why CCS is Not Like Reverse Gas Engineering
  • Deep geological storage of carbon dioxide (CO2), offshore UK: containment certainty
  • Risk sharing is vital for up scaling CCS, to combat climate change.
